Where's Waldo is a popular puzzle book series featuring a character in a red-and-white striped shirt, hidden in busy illustrations. Dungeons and Dragons (D&D) is a tabletop role-playing game where players embark on imaginative adventures, guided by a Dungeon Master who weaves a narrative. Players create unique characters with various abilities, races, and classes, working together to overcome challenges. The game is known for its rich storytelling, strategic combat, and vast world-building possibilities. Since its release in 1974, D&D has influenced countless other games and inspired a passionate global community of players.
